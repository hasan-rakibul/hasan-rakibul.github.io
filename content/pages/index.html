I'm glad you are here!

I'm a PhD candidate in Computing at 'Human-Centric Group in AI' at Curtin Perth campus. I'm fortunate to be supervised by Professor Tom Gedeon, Dr Md Zakir Hossain and Professor Aneesh Krishna, with whom I'm developing novel deep learning systems to detect empathy in human interactions. My overarching research interest includes advancing deep learning algorithms for natural language processing and computer vision.

In addition to research, I'm keen on teaching. I've been tutoring as a casual academic at Curtin University and Murdoch University. I'm on leave from BRAC University, Bangladesh, where I currently hold a senior lecturer position. I also lectured full-time at the Bangladesh Army International University of Science & Technology.

I received BSc (2019) and MSc (2021) degrees from Khulna University of Engineering & Technology, Bangladesh. In 2024, I received the 'Quality Journal Publication Award' from BRAC University for two of my publications between January and June 2023. I received a few grants, including a research grant in 2024-2025 from BRAC University and three compute grants in 2025 and 2024 from the Pawsey Supercomputing Research Centre, Australia. I championed the "Seeds for the Future 2018" program by Huawei Technologies Co. Ltd.

I am a big fan of open online learning and have completed over 25 certified courses on machine learning, deep learning and data science from Coursera, DataCamp and other similar platforms.
